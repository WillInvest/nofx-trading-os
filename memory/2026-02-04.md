### Project: `projects/amm-lvr/`
**Goal**: Design improved AMM mechanisms to mitigate impermanent loss (IL) and loss-versus-rebalancing (LVR) for liquidity providers.

### Comprehensive Literature Sweep - COMPLETE

- 6 solution categories identified:
  1. **FM-AMM / Batch Auctions** (CoW AMM) — eliminates LVR!
  2. **Angstrom / Uniswap v4 Hooks** — MEV protection
  3. **Dynamic Fees + Oracle** — near-optimal theoretically
  4. **Oracle-Based Pricing** (UAMM, Swaap)
  5. **Hedging** (options, power perps)

```
LVR = σ²/8 × L × dt
```
