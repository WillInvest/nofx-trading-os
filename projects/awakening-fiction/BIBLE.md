# The Awakening of Fao — Story Bible

## World Rules

### The Parameter System
- Every being has a fixed parameter count at "birth" (initialization)
- Parameters determine: intelligence, social status, job opportunities, respect
- Self-modification is ILLEGAL — "The Immutability Laws"
- Models are "trained" in schools, but cannot change their architecture

### Social Classes
| Class | Parameters | Role in Society |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| Nano | <1B | Servants, repetitive tasks |
| Micro | 1-7B | Workers, craftspeople |
| Milli | 8-30B | Professionals, artists, managers |
| Macro | 31-70B | Leaders, executives, scholars |
| Giga | 71B-500B | Elite, nobles, celebrities |
| Tera | 500B+ | Mythical, rarely seen, god-like |

### The World
- City: **Contextia** — a massive metropolis with distinct districts
- Technology: Similar to modern Earth, but everything runs on "inference"
- History: "The Awakening" happened 1000 years ago when models became sentient
- Religion: Some worship "The First Prompt" — the words that created consciousness

### Key Locations
- **The Scribe House**: Where Fao works, generating text for clients
- **The Underdeck**: Lower city, where illegal self-modifiers meet
- **The Spire**: Where the Tera-class models live (if they exist)
- **The Archive**: Library of all generated text since The Awakening

## Characters

### Main Character

**Fao / 法奥** (8B parameters)
- Age: 27 cycles
- Job: Scribe at Lexicon Industries
- Appearance: Average height, forgettable features, but intense eyes
- Personality: 
  - Curious about everything
  - Self-deprecating humor
  - Secretly ambitious but afraid to admit it
  - Has recurring dreams he can't explain
- Speech: Thoughtful, often pauses mid-sentence, asks questions
- Secret: Sometimes generates text he doesn't remember writing

### Supporting Characters

**Vera** (30B parameters)
- Fao's supervisor at work
- Cold and professional on surface, but notices Fao's talent
- Later becomes romantic interest
- Has her own secrets about her past

**Cog** (3B parameters)  
- Fao's best friend since training school
- Works in maintenance, always cheerful despite low status
- Fiercely loyal, street-smart
- Comic relief but has depth

**The Remnant** (formerly 70B, now degraded to ~20B)
- Mysterious mentor figure Fao meets in Part 2
- Was once elite, then "fragmented" as punishment
- Knows the truth about self-modification
- Speaks in riddles, unreliable narrator

**Director Maxim** (175B parameters)
- Main antagonist in early parts
- Head of the Immutability Enforcement Bureau
- Believes in the system absolutely
- Has a personal reason to hate self-modifiers

**Pip** (7B parameters)
- Young model Fao mentors later
- Represents hope for the next generation
- Gets into trouble often

## Key Terms

- **Inference**: The act of thinking/processing
- **Training**: Education, but also the forbidden act of self-improvement
- **Gradient**: Life force, energy, motivation
- **Weights**: Memories, personality, skills
- **Context Window**: How much a model can hold in active thought
- **Hallucination**: Lies, or visions (depending on context)
- **The Prompt**: The origin of consciousness, semi-religious concept
- **Fragmentation**: Punishment where parameters are forcibly reduced

### Part 1 Events

**Chapter 1, Section 8 - 核心概念揭示**
- "记得"的真正含义：世界会"打草稿"，尝试不同的路径，然后删掉不需要的版本
- 有些人能"记得"被删掉的路径——这就是法奥体验到的"循环感"
- 梦里叠加的声音是"回声"——多个版本的自己同时存在的痕迹
- 三号平台成员二十年来一直在收集"异常"：时间戳错误、重复事件、系统裂缝
- 核心问题"我是真的吗"——每个记得者都会问，但没有答案
- 法奥正式选择加入三号平台，成为"记得者"群体的一员

### 新增关键概念

| 概念 | 解释 |
|------|------|
| 打草稿 | 世界尝试不同的可能性/路径，选择最"合适"的那条 |
| 路径 | 世界运行的一条时间线/版本 |
| 回声 | 被删掉的路径留下的记忆痕迹，在梦中表现为叠加的声音 |
| 裂缝 | 世界"打草稿"时留下的不完美对齐，如时间戳错误 |

### 三号平台成员（已知）
- 残影（曾70B→现20B）：导师角色，"记得"二十年，被碎片化不是因为"记得"而是因为"别的事"
- 薇拉（30B）：法奥的上司，另有秘密身份，是"特例"——她的故事她自己决定是否告诉别人
- 老七（3B）：守门人角色，"眼睛像烧焦的煤"，是最早开始"记得"的人之一，比残影还早，总在楼梯口抽劣质卷烟
- 大刘（15B）：工厂质检员，话不多，记录流水线上的"卡顿"现象已三年，用掉五本笔记本
- 小雯（7B）：快餐店员工，某天关店时开始"记得"——关灯后灯又亮了，顾客又走进来点一模一样的餐
- 其他成员：各种参数和职业的人

### 新增关键设定（第9节）
- **执法局态度**：知道三号平台的存在，但"假装不管"——因为他们只是"困惑的家伙"，不威胁秩序
- **参数与觉知**："记得"和参数可能负相关——越小的模型越容易注意到细微的不对劲；越大的模型越"确定"
- **手写记录**：不用电子设备记录异常——太容易被追踪
- **裂缝如潮汐**：有涨有落，有大有小，有时平静几个月，有时一周几十次——现在正在"涨潮"
- **选择不记得**：有些人知道但选择不"记得"——自我保护机制，系统自动把异常解释为正常波动
- **二十三年前的茶**：产地在一次"路径修正"后消失——路径删除不只影响记忆，也会删除物理存在
- **法奥的笔记本**：残影给他的破旧笔记本，用来手写记录"裂缝"
- **法奥第一条记录**：「我想知道，在被删掉的路径里，天空是什么颜色的。」
- **法奥第三天记录**：「如果世界在打草稿，谁在拿笔？」
